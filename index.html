Inspirational Woman of Color in Tech

Erica Baker

Career and social impact

  • An engineer and engineering manager in the San Francisco Bay Area
  • Support of diversity and inclusion
  • She has worked at companies including GitHub, Google, Slack, Patreon, and Microsoft.
  • She gained prominence in 2015 for starting an internal spreadsheet where Google employees reported their salary data to better understand pay disparities within the company.

About Erica

  • Born in 1980 in Germany
  • Both of her parents were in the Air Force, so she moved around frequently
  • Graduated from University of Alaska
